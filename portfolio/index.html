About Me

I began my career as a broadcast editor, where I developed a strong sense of structuring messages and shaping flow.

I excel at quickly identifying problems and translating practical solutions into visual form.

Today, I design clear and intentional digital experiences grounded in user context.

Experience

2013 - 2018

Worked as a broadcast post-production director,
overseeing the entire technical process to shape visuals and deliver key messages.
Responsible for all technical quality excluding content editing
— including subtitling, colour correction, timing, sound, and final output.
Collaborated closely with various departments such as brand design teams, executing the final production and implementation based on completed designs.

2019 - 2023

Worked as a barista and store manager in Korea and the UK,
where I developed strong awareness and responsiveness to user behaviour by engaging with a wide range of customers.

2024 – Present

Transitioned into a UX/UI designer, focusing on designing digital products rooted in brand experience.
Strong in information architecture and user flow design,
and also equipped with practical web publishing knowledge and implementation skills.

Strengths

Experienced in information architecture and user flow design.

Capable of logical design at the system and UI component level.

Understand the full process from planning to design to publishing.

Create outcomes that balance functionality and visual clarity.

Off the Record

My favourite ice cream brand during overseas trips had plenty of charm,

but its value wasn’t effectively communicated in Korea — this became the starting point of the Ben & Jerry’s redesign.

A simple empathy — “I love animals” — grew into a deeper reflection on how we share information,

which eventually took form as the KARMA web & mobile renewal project.

My experience at a K-pop club in London, where I connected with friends from diverse backgrounds,

led me to reframe fan activity with a more open and structured approach — which inspired the Jennie fan page design.
