  • A Scoop of Flavour, A Scoop of Purpose

    Ben & Jerry’s began in 1978 as a small ice cream shop founded by two friends, Ben Cohen and Jerry Greenfield, in Burlington, Vermont. With bold flavours and generous chocolate chunks, it quickly became one of the most iconic premium ice cream brands worldwide.

    But what makes Ben & Jerry’s truly special goes beyond taste alone. The brand is committed to social and environmental good — from sourcing Fairtrade ingredients, to using non-GMO products,
    and supporting animal welfare with cage-free eggs.

    Believing in “more than just ice cream,”
    Ben & Jerry’s continues to serve scoops
    that make the world a little better.
